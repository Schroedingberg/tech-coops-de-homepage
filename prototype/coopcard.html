Slobodna Domena

Democratic and socially responsible worker owned organisation.

Slobodna domena, open source and design cooperative is a transparent, democratic and socially responsible organization, founded to set up and support various initiatives with the common goal of defending and expanding the definition of common goods, the just distribution of surplus values, promoting equality, fighting discrimination and nurturing democratic decision-making. It gathers designers, programmers, engineers and other creative individuals with the common goal of providing complete professional services.

  • Web Portals, Apps and Services
  • Mobile Development
  • Branding and Identity
  • Illustration

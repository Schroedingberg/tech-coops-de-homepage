Who We Are

Patio is a global community of worker-owned cooperatives from all over the world, specializing in the development of digital technology, communication, and design. Our federation offers a supportive and collaborative network of like-minded cooperatives committed to social responsibility, democratic decision-making, and a new world beyond corporate capitalism. As a Patio member, you will have access to a variety of benefits, including shared resources, peer-to-peer learning opportunities, and a platform to showcase your cooperative's work. We collaborate by sharing knowledge, job opportunities, and human resources. Patio provides the best experience for both co-op members and our partners.
